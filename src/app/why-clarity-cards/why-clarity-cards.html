Why ClarityCheck

Accuracy

  • Multi‑agent cross‑verification for fewer false positives
  • RAG with FAISS vectors to ground outputs in your sources
  • Deterministic prompts + guardrails to reduce drift

Speed

  • Parallel agent analysis for low end‑to‑end latency
  • Chunked ingestion and cached embeddings
  • Responsive UI with streaming progress

Security & Privacy

  • Encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Storage policy: retain up to 3 PDFs on Free; easy deletion
  • Data retention: short‑lived caches; no training on your data
